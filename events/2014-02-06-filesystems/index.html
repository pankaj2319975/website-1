Filesystems

1 minute read Published: 6 Feb, 2014

This Thursday, February 6th at 7:00pm in Caldwell Labs 120, Paul Schellin will present “Filesystems”

This topic will cover: - A history on filesystems - Examples of current and past FSs - Virtual filesystems - A custom-built filesystem - anything else!

Laptops are encouraged though not required, and as always, there will be pizza.
