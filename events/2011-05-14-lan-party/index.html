LAN Party

    On Saturday, May 14, from 10:00AM to 11:30PM in the Ohio Union Creative Arts room. The emphasis will be on Free, Open Source games, but people are welcome to play whatever they’d like. Additional details can be found at the LAN website as well as a registration form, to help us get a feel for how many people we will have and what games you want to play. Please bring your computer, any game media you might need, as well as a network cable or two, in case some people do not have one. If you have any questions, please send an email to lan@opensource.osu.edu.
